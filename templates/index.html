PROJECT SCOPE

Can machine learning algorithms (using production and consumption data gathered from the Energy Information Administration (EIA) database) help predict energy production and consumption in California and Texas; two (2) states that have different perspectives and practices pertaining to energy extraction and utilization in the US and global energy landscape.

Data Source

EIA State Energy Data System (SEDS): 1960-2017

Program and Libraries

Python (TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Pandas, Matplotlib, Numpy, Flask), JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Heroku, Github, Excel.

Machine Learning Models

Linear Regression: Not ideal for analysis; No strong correlations between features and labels.
LSTM (Long short-term memory) artificial recurrent neural network: Gave desired results .

Results

Prediction of energy production and consumption in California and Texas from years 2018 to 2040. Time will tell…...
